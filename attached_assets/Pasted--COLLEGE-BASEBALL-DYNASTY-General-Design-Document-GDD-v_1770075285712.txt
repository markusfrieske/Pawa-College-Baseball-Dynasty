⚾ COLLEGE BASEBALL DYNASTY
General Design Document (GDD – v1.0)
1. PRODUCT VISION
High Concept

A league-first, story-driven college baseball dynasty simulator where human coaches compete in recruiting, roster management, and long-term program building—while playing actual games in Power Pros.

The app is the single source of truth for:

teams

rosters

recruiting

eligibility

transfers

schedules

standings

history

Power Pros is used only to play games.

Core Fantasy

You are the Head Coach + Athletic Director of a college baseball program, competing against other humans and CPU teams in a shared league where information, relationships, and decisions matter as much as wins.

What Makes This Game Different

This is not a spreadsheet sim.
This is three engines interacting:

Recruiting Engine – points, fog of war, hidden thresholds

Story Engine – branching, probabilistic narratives that alter reality

Human Engine – how real people react to partial information
