## Target Product Design

The League Hub should feel like a sports video game command center, not a generic admin dashboard.

The first screen should answer:

1. What league, season, week, and phase am I in?
2. What team am I coaching?
3. What is my record and competitive position?
4. What is the next thing I need to do?
5. Am I ready? If not, why?
6. Who is my next opponent or what phase objective is active?
7. What recruiting/roster issue needs attention?
8. What just happened around the league?
