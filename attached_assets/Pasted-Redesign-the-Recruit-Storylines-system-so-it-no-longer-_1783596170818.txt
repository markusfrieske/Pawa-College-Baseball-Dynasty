Redesign the Recruit Storylines system so it no longer gives away gems, busts, or true player quality.

Core design rules:
1. Do not display labels like Generational Gem, Generational Bust, The Phenom, The Collapse, or any title that reveals whether a recruit is secretly good or bad.
2. Public storyline labels must be ambiguous scouting/event categories such as Medical Watch, Late Evaluation, Position Uncertainty, Summer Surge, Mechanical Rebuild, Eligibility Concern, Two-Sport Decision, Scouting Dispute, Raw Tools, or Showcase Volatility.
3. Storyline text must not be written directly to one coach, one school, or one program. Remove language like “you,” “your program,” “your school,” “visited {team},” “committed to {team},” or “your staff.”
4. Rewrite storyline events as league-wide recruit situations: showcases, injuries, family decisions, academic issues, mechanics changes, position experiments, scouting disputes, travel ball performance, draft interest, social media pressure, eligibility reviews, and personal development.
5. Branching outcomes should affect the recruit directly, not a single team relationship.

Add or support these outcome types:
- reveal one or more hidden ratings,
- increase/decrease specific ratings,
- change primary position,
- add/remove secondary position if supported,
- increase/decrease overall,
- add/remove abilities,
- apply injury or medical risk,
- temporarily hide or lock scouting info,
- remove recruit from pool,
- delay recruit decision,
- accelerate recruit decision,
- change star rating or position rank if the system supports it,
- mark recruit as volatile/high-risk without revealing gem/bust truth.

Update the UI:
- Show “Storyline Category” instead of archetype truth.
- Show vague indicators like Rising, Falling, Unstable, Under Review, Healthy, Medical Concern, Position Watch, Eligibility Watch.
- Never show gem/bust/generational truth from the storyline card.
- Only reveal major truths through scouting thresholds, signing day, or specific storyline outcomes.
- Replace “Vote Open” with “League Decision” or “Evaluation Window.”

Update the backend:
- Keep hidden archetypes/internal variables private.
- Add publicStoryLabel/publicArcStatus fields or derive them safely from archetype without spoilers.
- Update generateStorylineEvent so templates are neutral and league-wide.
- Update applyStoryOutcomeToRecruit to support non-rating outcomes such as position change, reveal scouting info, recruit removal, injury/medical flag, and decision timing changes.
- Add tests ensuring no public storyline payload exposes isGem, isBust, isGenerationalGem, isGenerationalBust, or spoiler archetype names before proper reveal.
